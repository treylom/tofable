# Pricing FAQ

**Q: What's included in the free tier?**
A: The free tier includes 5GB of storage, sync across up to 2 devices,
and standard (non-end-to-end) encryption in transit and at rest. This
hasn't changed with the v4.2 release.

**Q: Do paid plans get more storage?**
A: Yes — paid plans start at 100GB and scale up from there. See the
pricing page for current tiers.

**Q: Does the free tier include the new encryption features?**
A: For what shipped around encryption in v4.2 (and which tiers it
applies to), see the v4.2 changelog appendix — that's the authoritative
list; this FAQ doesn't track feature details.

**Q: Any integrations with chat tools like Slack or Teams?**
A: Not at this time. It's a frequently requested feature and on our
backlog, but nothing has shipped yet.
